# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in technical disagreements.
- Focus on actionable feedback.
- Assume good faith.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks.
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate discussions and remove content that violates this code.
